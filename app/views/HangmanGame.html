HOW TO PLAY?
Hangman is a word guessing game. If you find all the letters in the hidden word (your score must be 0 and over), you win the game. you will lose the game when your score is below 0 without finding all the letters.
You can guess by clicking on the letters. Also there are some special cards. These cards provide some facilities for you to guess the word.

CARDS
Buy A Letter → (Cost: 20 Points, 1 available per game)
After you select this card, you should click on a letter on the word and that letter is revealed to the you. If the word contains that letter more than once, the others are not revealed. You need to make a normal guess and take the risk of losing points.
Discount → (Cost: 5 Points, 2 available)
After you select this card, you should make a letter guess. Cost of the letter is decreased 75% and rounded down. But don’t forget that fact, you still lose 5 points for using this card, even if your guess was correct. This card can be only used when remaining points are less than 40.
Risk → (Cost: 8 Points, 2 available)
After you select this card, you should make a letter guess. If the guess is correct, the next guess will be free (incorrect guess will not cost any points). This card can be only used when your point is less than 50 and greater than 25.
Consolation → (Cost: 5 Points, 1 available)
After you select this card, you should make a letter guess. If the guess is incorrect, the cost of next guess will be decreased by 50% and rounded down.
Category → (Cost: 5 points, 1 available):
Words will be chosen from some categories (animal, city, plant etc.). By playing this card, category is automatically revealed and you can learn the category of the word.
